Welcome to Powerhouse Gym

Welcome to Powerhouse Gym in Durres, located at Rruga Sabri Tuci 2001. We're more than just a gym ,we're a supportive community dedicated to helping you reach your fitness goals.

Our gym is equipped with top-of-the-line machines and staffed by a friendly team ready to assist you. Whether you're new to fitness or a seasoned athlete, our inclusive environment welcomes everyone.

Join us to experience the benefits of regular exercise and a supportive community. At Powerhouse Gym, we're here to help you on your journey to a healthier lifestyle.
